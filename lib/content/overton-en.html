Why this concept today?

The Overton Window is one of the most useful tools for understanding how societies change, not through sudden revolution, but through the gradual shift of what is thinkable. In 2026, this mechanism is more visible than ever.

30
years separate "unthinkable" from "public policy" for mass digital surveillance. That was yesterday.

Dystopia in the 1990s, today it is legal infrastructure in most democracies. Not because citizens have radically changed their minds. Simply because the window of what is thinkable has shifted in stages, each making the next one possible.

The mechanism is not conspiracy. It is structural. And understanding it is to equip oneself to recognise it when it operates, on any subject.
