The World of Predators

Three empires, three doctrines of expansion. The undisguised return of spheres of influence.

In 2026, the United States, Russia, and China divide the world according to three radically different logics. Dominate, conquer, control.

We do not live in a multipolar world. We live in a three-empire world that divides zones of influence among itself, and calls that the international order.

China practices a multidimensional and patient expansion: investments in Africa and Central Asia via the Belt and Road, construction of artificial islands in the South China Sea, "debt trap" diplomacy, control of rare resources. Power extends without declaration of war.

Strategic control zones

China does not seek hegemony. It simply seeks to occupy the place that history has reserved for it.

Xi Jinping, CPC Congress speech, 2022

Dystopia in the 1990s, today it is legal infrastructure in most democracies. Not because citizens radically changed their minds. Simply because the window of what is thinkable shifted by degrees, each step making the next one possible.

The mechanism is not conspiracy. It is structural. And understanding it is what equips us to recognize it when it operates, on any subject.
